Join Fleek and a16z for a one-day hackathon to build vision-language and LLM-powered tools that make circular fashion work at scale. Spend the day building with mentors from the Fleek product and engineering teams, ship a working demo, and compete for Cursor prizes.
Secondhand is the fastest-growing corner of fashion - and the hardest to scale. Every item is one-of-a-kind: a single photo, no SKU, no clean data. New retail is a solved problem; secondhand is wide open. It's exactly the kind of messy, visual, real-world challenge that modern AI was built for.

The Challenge: pick a track
🔍 Vision Vision-language models for the hard parts of resale: cataloging, grading, authentication, defect detection, and visual search across millions of unique items.
🤖 Agents & LLMs Agentic and LLM-powered tools for sourcing, pricing, GTM tooling, negotiations, customer experience, and ops automation.
♻️ Circular Moonshot Open wildcard - anything that pushes the circular economy forward: impact measurement, waste reduction, or reimagining how people buy and sell secondhand.

FAQ
Do I need a team? No - come solo or with up to four. We run team formation in the morning.
How technical do I need to be? This is a builder's hackathon - you should be able to ship something in a day. Designers and PMs are welcome on teams.
Who owns what I build? You own your work.
Is it really free? Yes. Capacity is limited and registration is approval-based, so RSVP early.
